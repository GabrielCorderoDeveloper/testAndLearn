About me

Hey!!!

Hi, I am Gabriel, I am someone passionate about programming and learning new things. My story begins 7 years ago, my first coding experience was creating modifications for a game called Minecraft in its mobile version, I learned the basics of JavaScript by trial and error just changing things and seeing what happens, that was the beginning for my passion of programming and learning.

When I have an objective, I do everything I can to achieve that goal. At the age of 16 I decided that I wanted to learn how to draw, and it took me over 2 years to be good enough at drawing. In my drawing journey I learned that the most important thing is perseverance, working every day is the keystone of self-taught learning and today I can proudly say that I am on my way to be a self-taught front-end developer improving every single day to reach my maximum potential.
